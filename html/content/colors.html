Colors

10 new concept icons will be debuted with a new gradient color scheme to unify the brand and get away from our current rainbow palette. The current color scheme is mismatched and does not unify our branding. This change will help make the brand look more clean/mature and helps the icons look like a collection when seen together. The range provides connection between the ten concepts themselves, visually bringing to light how they build upon one and other to enhance human health. Generally blue is used to suggest trust and is often used by more corporate and conservative brands. Green shades are used to promote health, tranquility, relaxation, power, and nature. While blue will still be our main color, the addition of green will help us promote these facets of our brand as well.
